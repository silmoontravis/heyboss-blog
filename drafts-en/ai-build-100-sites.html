How to Build 100 Brand Websites in One Day with AI — Full Process Revealed

Travis Chen 2026-04-18 8 min read

A traditional web design agency typically needs 1-3 months and a budget of NT$50,000-300,000 to build a single brand website. Designer mockups, front-end development, rounds of revisions — every step piles on more labor and time.

But at HEY!BOSS 銀月數位顧問, we broke through that barrier with AI — actually completing 100 brand websites in a single day. These aren't cookie-cutter template sites. Each one features unique brand design, a custom logo, background music (BGM), and bilingual content in Chinese and English.

This article reveals our complete AI website building process.

1. The Core AI Website Building Process

The entire workflow is divided into 5 automated stages:

  1. Brand Data Generation — AI automatically generates brand names, brand stories, service offerings, and color schemes based on industry categories
  2. Automatic Logo Design — AI generates logos based on brand identity, including primary color and white background versions
  3. BGM Background Music — AI music generation tools create unique background music for each brand
  4. Website Construction — AI generates complete HTML/CSS websites with bilingual Chinese/English switching based on brand data
  5. Automated Deployment — CI/CD pipeline automatically deploys to production, including SSL certificates and domain configuration

2. What Does Each Website Include?

Don't assume AI-built means low quality. Each of our brand websites includes:

  • Unique Brand Design — Custom color palette, typography, and visual style that won't look like any other brand
  • Professional Logo — AI-generated brand logos, not icon collages
  • BGM Background Music — Playable/pausable brand background music for enhanced immersion
  • Bilingual Support — One-click Chinese/English switching with fully bilingual content
  • Mobile Optimization — Full RWD responsive design, perfect on phones, tablets, and desktops
  • SEO Infrastructure — Meta tags, JSON-LD structured data, and Open Graph tags all in place

3. How Is Quality Assured?

Speed doesn't mean sacrificing quality. We use the Playwright automated testing framework to conduct three rounds of quality audits on all websites:

  • Round 1: Page loading, HTTP status codes, image integrity
  • Round 2: Mobile compatibility, horizontal overflow detection
  • Round 3: Post-fix regression testing to ensure zero issues

Final result: All 100 websites passed the quality audit — zero broken images, zero 404s, zero JavaScript errors.

4. Deployment and SEO Are Automated Too

After the websites are built, what's next? The traditional process still requires: buying hosting, configuring DNS, applying for SSL, manually uploading files, submitting sitemaps... any step could become a bottleneck.

At HEY!BOSS, all of this is automated:

  • Jenkins CI/CD — Code push triggers automatic build, test, and deploy — live in 2 minutes
  • SSL Certificates — Let's Encrypt automatic issuance and renewal
  • SEO Monitoring — Google Search Console auto-integration, automatic sitemap submission
  • Daily Reports — Keyword ranking changes pushed to Telegram daily, no manual checking needed

5. What Does This Mean for Your Business?

If you're a business that needs a brand website, AI website building means:

  • Time: From months down to days, or even hours
  • Cost: Significantly reduced — savings can be redirected to marketing
  • Quality: Automated testing ensures consistently high quality
  • SEO: Complete SEO infrastructure from day one
  • Maintenance: Automated monitoring catches issues before they become problems
