The First Step in Digital Transformation for Taiwan's SMEs

Travis Chen 2026-04-22 8 min read

Taiwan has over 1.6 million small and medium enterprises (SMEs), accounting for more than 97% of all businesses. These enterprises form the backbone of Taiwan's economy, yet their digitalization progress is generally lagging behind.

It's not that business owners don't want to digitalize — they just don't know where to start, how much it will cost, or who to work with. In this article, HEY!BOSS 銀月數位顧問 takes a no-nonsense approach to explain how to take that crucial first step toward digital transformation.

1. Why Do SMEs Need Digital Transformation?

Here's a harsh reality: your customers are already digital. They use Google to search for restaurants, LINE to book services, and Instagram to check reviews. If you don't exist in these spaces, you simply don't exist.

Concrete benefits of digital transformation:

  • Be found — When customers search for your industry keywords, your brand appears in search results
  • Build trust — A professional brand website is more convincing than a business card
  • Reduce costs — Online bookings replace phone calls, automation replaces repetitive manual tasks
  • Expand your market — From neighborhood customers to city-wide or even nationwide reach
  • Data-driven decisions — Know where customers come from, what they view, how long they stay, and optimize operations with data

2. The Five Major Obstacles to SME Digital Transformation

Based on our experience serving hundreds of SMEs, here are the most common obstacles we hear:

Obstacle 1: "It's too expensive"

Traditional web agencies commonly quote NT$100,000-300,000, which is a significant burden for SMEs with monthly revenues of NT$500,000-1,000,000. But this obstacle is being eliminated by AI technology — HEY!BOSS's AI website building delivers the same quality at far below traditional prices.

Obstacle 2: "I don't understand technology"

Business owners don't need to understand technology. A good digital consultant communicates in language you understand. You just need to tell us your brand story and business needs — we handle the technology.

Obstacle 3: "I don't have time"

SME owners are busy around the clock. Where's the time to go back and forth with a designer? HEY!BOSS's AI website building process is extremely streamlined — one interview to gather requirements, AI auto-generates, you just confirm and fine-tune.

Obstacle 4: "It won't make a difference"

Many owners have had painful experiences: spent a fortune on a website, only to get a handful of visitors per year. The problem isn't the website itself — it's that SEO wasn't done. HEY!BOSS's service includes SEO infrastructure and daily monitoring, ensuring your website actually gets seen.

Obstacle 5: "My competitors haven't done it either"

That's actually your opportunity! If your competitors haven't digitalized yet, taking this step first means you dominate the search results. By the time competitors react, you've already built up SEO authority and brand trust.

3. The Right Sequence for Digital Transformation

Digital transformation isn't a one-step process, but there is a correct sequence:

  1. Brand website — This is the foundation for all digital marketing. Without a website, everything else is just talk
  2. Google Business Profile — Let local customers find you directly through search
  3. Social media — Facebook, Instagram, LINE Official Account — choose your focus platform based on industry
  4. SEO optimization — Improve your brand website's ranking in search results
  5. Automation tools — Online reservations, auto-replies, automated report generation

Note: Website first, then everything else. Running ads without a website is like pouring money down the drain — customers click the ad but have no place to learn about your brand.
